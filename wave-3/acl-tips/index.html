5

tips to recover from ACL surgery

1 Manage
your pain

Controlling your pain will help you complete your physical therapy and accelerate recovery. While some pain and swelling are to be expected, acetaminophen and anti-inflammatories are effective for minimizing discomfort.

schedule a checkup »

2 Start your rehab
as soon as you can

Patients who start rehab in the first three days often fare better in terms of pain management, muscle recovery, and returning to their daily activities and independence.

schedule an appointment »

3 Listen to your
health team

Follow all of your medical instructions, including watching for any potential complications, such as a fever exceeding 101.5°.

talk to a specialist »

4 Keep active

Typically, it’s good to return to daily activities right away. Driving, working and taking walks help you recover. Just avoid sports and pivoting activities until advised by your doctor.

schedule a checkup »

5 Set recovery
goals for yourself

Your goal during rehab and recovery is to manage your symptoms and regain pain-free knee strength and function. Talk to your medical team about the best strategies and exercises needed to achieve your goals.
